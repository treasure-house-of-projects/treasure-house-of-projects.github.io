Who we are

Treasure House of Projects (NGO) unlocks creative potential across society. We work in charity, volunteering, health, culture, art, youth initiatives, education and social protection.

Main activities

  • Information & awareness programmes
  • Advocacy and rights protection
  • Fund-raising for social impact
  • Training and capacity-building
  • Events, festivals and community gatherings
  • Educational outreach & enlightenment

Flagship projects

  • “Latusho” Book Club – uniting book enthusiasts of every age
  • “Yujin Fest” – yearly festival promoting healthy lifestyles
  • “Street Music Fest” – discover tomorrow’s headline musicians
  • Skarb NGO platform – matching nonprofits with IT volunteers
